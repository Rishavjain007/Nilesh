About Our Company

Building excellence since 2008 with quality, commitment, and expertise.

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by Director Nilesh, Nilesh Construction began as a small building material supply business with a vision to provide quality materials at fair prices. Over the years, we expanded our services to include complete construction solutions, leveraging our material expertise to ensure quality at every stage.

Today, we're a full-service construction and building material company with a team of 50+ skilled professionals, having successfully completed over 300 projects across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Our growth is built on a foundation of trust, quality, and customer satisfaction. We believe that strong relationships with clients, suppliers, and our team are key to delivering exceptional results.

Commitment to Quality & Safety

Quality Assurance

Our quality commitment begins with material selection and continues through every construction phase:

  • Source materials only from certified, reputable suppliers
  • Regular material testing in certified laboratories
  • Strict adherence to IS codes and building regulations
  • Daily site inspections by qualified supervisors
  • Final quality audit before project handover

Safety First Approach

We prioritize the safety of our workers, clients, and the public:

  • Mandatory safety training for all workers
  • Provision of proper safety gear and equipment
  • Regular safety audits and hazard assessments
  • Compliance with OSHA and local safety regulations
  • Emergency response plans for all sites
